Re-Engineering

Event Details.

Re Engineering relates to constructing a new part with the existing design details. With the help of this event we meet general engineering outcomes like Engineering Knowledge, Modern Tool Usage, and Team Work. The event consists of two rounds. In the First Round one of the team member will be blind folded and will select components from a box in which components related to a simple machine will be kept. The team which will assemble the components to form the given machine in least possible time will advance to the next round. In the Second Round both components and steps to assemble will be given. The team with most accurate assembly and least time will win.

Clash of Brains

Event Details.

Event Details: This will be a technical quiz covering wide engineering topics like Thermal, Design, Production, Electrical, Electronics, Computers, Sustainability etc. The quiz will meet general engineering outcomes like Engineering Knowledge, Environment and Sustainability and Communication. The event consists of three rounds, in the first round contestants will have to pick up genres placed in a bowl. The team which writes most number of correct answer advances. The second round will be round. The third round will be rapid fire.
